Little Something About KLM Design

KLM Design is graphic designer Kimberly McCormick. I stumbled onto graphic design while working for glass artist Milon Townsend at Townsend Associates. I began working on the layout of his first book Advanced Flameworking Volume I, an almost 200-page step-by-step guide to flameworking techniques with hundreds of images and illustrations. I color corrected all those images, laid out every page, inputted and proofread the text too many times to count.

After the completion of the book, Milon opened Blue Moon Press to sell books and videos about flameworking. I became the Executive Director of Blue Moon Press and was responsible for processing all orders, maintaining the website while producing 3 more books, multiple booklets, flyers, magazine ads and video covers.

Near the end of my 5-year career with Blue Moon Press, I went to Monroe Community College to study graphic design. I received an A.A.S Visual Communication: Graphic Arts/Printing degree in 2003.

I am currently attending Polk State College, updating and learning skills for this ever-changing industry. I enjoy getting my hands dirty producing outdoor Halloween themes at my home for my housing community every year. When not working on projects, I am out geocaching or playing Pokémon™ Go.
