Check out my Halloween themes by year.

2020 - Science

The sounds draw the treaters in; Weird Science, Somebody's Watching Me, The Monster Mash, Purple People Eater, The Big Bang Theory Theme Song, She Blinded Me with Science and Stuck to You by Josh Ritter. Start walking towards the house with a sign stating Hyde Laboratories above a garage door featuring multiple chalkboards. Next, are crazy steampunk machines littering the yard and walkway to the front door. The machines have working dials, fans, and switches. Don't forget the plasma ball!

I built Telsa coils to power Frankenstein's monster. Once there is a knock at the door, the treaters are greeted by a Dr. Jekyll and Ms. Hyde. A half and half hand-made outfit with corresponding mask, hard at work writing in their journal and concocting experiments. The lab foyer features bottles, bubbling liquids, and petri dishes. Candy is given out with long tongs. Hurry and get your treat before you become an experiment.

2018 - Alice In Wonderland

Welcome to Alice's Wonderland! Begin your journey playing a game of chess that can even climb up the garage door. Maybe you fancy a game of croquet instead? If you have built up a thirst, sit down at the table for a spot of tea and celebrate your un-birthday.

Take the walkway towards the house through the mushroom to get to the treats. On your way stop and say hi to the flowers and the 6-foot caterpillar. Which door is the right one? The Queen of Hearts is literally decked out from head to toe in face cards. You better play no tricks here or it will be…

off with your head!
