Rook Magazine Cover

Queen of Hearts Card

A mock magazine featuring traditional games. This was a special edition devoted to playing cards.

The entire piece was created using Adobe® Photoshop® software. This piece was a college project for the class Image Editing in Photoshop®. I wanted the layout to mimic a traditional face playing card. I created the playing card dress and crown in 2018, as part of my Alice in Wonderland Halloween theme.
